Understanding this calculator is pretty simple:

  1. It let's you input number ranging from 0-9 as indivdual numbers or digits of some number.
  2. C button clears all the entries and wipes out any existing data.
  3. "+","-","*", and "/" lets you add, subtract,multiply, and divide respectively.
  4. The calculator lets you string together several operations and get the right answer.
  5. The calculator doesn't not evaluate more than a single pair of numbers at a time.
  6. The calculator rounds answers with long decimals so that they don't overflow the screen.
  7. Pressing "=" before entering all of the numbers or an operator could cause you problems!
